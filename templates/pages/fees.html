Insurance

I am an out-of-network provider and do not bill insurance directly. I provide a monthly superbill that you can submit to your insurance carrier for potential reimbursement. Please contact your insurance provider for details about your out-of-network mental health benefits.

Cancellation Policy

I require 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice are charged at the full session rate. This policy exists to protect time that has been held specifically for you and your partner.

Getting Started

The first step is a free 30-minute phone consultation. You describe what's going on; I ask a few questions; we determine whether working together makes sense. From there we begin with joint sessions — you, your partner, and me. Sessions can take place in my office in San Leandro, via video with both of you at the same location, or with each of you joining from a separate screen. Individual sessions with each partner are not a standard part of my process, though I'm happy to arrange them if requested. I expect both partners to feel genuinely understood and to have a clear picture of the relational dynamic within the first two sessions.
